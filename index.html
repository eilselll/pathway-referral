Project 2 — Referral infrastructure

Referrals are generated.
Then they disappear.

41% of referred patients never book. Not because they don't want care — but because the system drops them the moment they leave the room. This prototype fixes what happens after the referral is created.

Rationale + walkthrough

Why this changes behavior,
not just tracking

The existing system records that a referral was made. This system ensures it actually happens — by intervening at every point where patients drop off.

Where drop-off is reduced
01
Patient forgets before they get home
The SMS arrives within minutes of the consultation ending — while the doctor's recommendation is still fresh. Booking takes 60 seconds from the link.
Timing intervention
02
Patient doesn't know who to see or how to get there
Nearest providers are surfaced automatically with distance, availability, and billing information. Directly addresses the barrier for older and underserved patients.
Geography intervention
03
Patient means to book but delays indefinitely
An automated 48hr SMS nudge re-surfaces the booking link for patients who opened it but didn't complete. No doctor action required.
Friction intervention
04
Doctor never knows what happened
Every step is tracked and surfaced in the referring doctor's dashboard. Attended, missed, outcome received. The loop closes automatically.
Loop closure
How context is preserved across handoffs
AI transcript → structured referral
Clinical context flows directly from the consultation into the referral. The doctor reviews and edits — nothing is sent without approval.
Referral → patient booking page
The booking page shows the patient exactly why they were referred, in plain language. Reduces the "is this really necessary?" hesitation that causes silent drop-off.
Referral → specialist context packet
The receiving practitioner gets structured clinical notes, findings, goals, and patient context before the appointment. No cold starts.
Outcome → referring doctor
After the appointment, the specialist sends a brief outcome update. The referring doctor's loop closes automatically.
Before vs. after
Referral created→Patient leaves→Silence→41% never book
Referral created→SMS in minutes→Book in 60 sec→Specialist briefed→Loop closed
The key design principle: every intervention removes a decision the patient has to make themselves. The system does the work — finding providers, sending reminders, closing the loop — so patients don't have to.
Designed for all three stakeholders
Referring doctor
AI drafts, doctor approves. Full visibility from referral to outcome. Zero extra admin.
Patient
SMS only. No app, no account. Plain language. Nearest provider shown. Works on any phone.
Specialist
Full context before the visit. No blind referrals. Knows who sent it and why.
